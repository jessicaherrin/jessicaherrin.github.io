AI Strategy Recommendation

Shift your growth mix to 70/30 newness-to-promo. Your 2024 data shows you ran promos on 54 of 365 days (15%) but they only contributed $321K vs $1.3M from new products. The math is clear: every dollar invested in new product development returns 2× what a dollar of promo discounting returns, with better margin. Reserve promos for clearing aged Sell Down inventory (currently 28% of mix — above your 15-25% target) and time your biggest drops to April and September when your launch scores peak.

Optimal cadence: 4-6 new SKUs per month (your sweet spot from Jan data), 1 promo event per quarter (max 5 days), 1 GWP campaign per season. This preserves brand equity while keeping the newness engine running.
