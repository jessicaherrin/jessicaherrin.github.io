Conversion Gap

Social traffic is up 31% but your conversion rate dropped to 0.9% — the lowest of any channel. For context, your email converts at 3.2% and paid at 2.1%. The issue isn't traffic quality — your social visitors browse 3.4 pages/session (above average). They're browsing but not buying. Two likely causes: (1) your social landing experience sends visitors to the homepage instead of the product featured in the post, and (2) only 4 of your 22 Instagram posts have shoppable product tags. Posts with tags convert 3.2x higher.

What to do: Tag every post with products. Use UTM links that deep-link to the featured product page, not your homepage. If you're running a retargeting campaign from social visitors, make sure the ad shows the exact product they viewed — not a generic brand ad.
